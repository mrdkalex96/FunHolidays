ABOUT US

FUNHOLIDAYS is a travel company based in London (uk) specialised in providing the best deals to travel around Europe.
We are here to help you finding your perfect europe deal.

FH was founded in February 2006 by William Moore and Luke Hawkins.
At the moment the company is based in one branch that you can find on Liverpool Street.

Our team is one of the best that you can find, please come to our branch and you will find out yourself.
